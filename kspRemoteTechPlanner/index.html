Visual RemoteTech Planner for KSP

Description

RemoteTech2 is popular MOD for KerbalSpaceProgram. It provides purpose to launch and use satellites in the world of KSP.
Before launching your communication satellite, you can plan your RemoteTech satellites network with graphical figures here. Put needed values into Data input, then hit Calculate.

Black circle is orbit of satellites and black dots mean each satellite. Red clear circle is antenna range each satellite has.
If arrow connecting a couple of satellites is blue, it'll be OK. But if it's red, any satellites can't connect to their next one.
When green circle labeled Stable appears, it means that any orbits lower than Stable altitude never have connection-less flight.

This website is under development, forum is here and sources are available on github. Current version is beta2.4.
Issues, bug reports, contributers are always welcome.

Data of bodies is based on KSP Wiki, RemoteTech2 manual and metaphor's Delta-V map.
